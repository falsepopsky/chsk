# HeatMapSurface

`HeatMapSurface` draws a rectangle on a heat map.

## Expansions

`HeatMapSurface` draws a rectangle with corners at the centers of heat map cells. To expand the rectangle to capture the entire cells, use prop `expansion` and provide values as multiples of cell width/height.

## Data subsets

Providing values to props `ids` and `keys` makes the surface cover only a part of the heatmap.
